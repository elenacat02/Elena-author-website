Hi! I’m Elena, an illustrator and writer creating art and stories for kids who love ​ adventure, animals, and a touch of mystery.

Whether I’m painting with watercolors or working digitally, I strive to bring as ​much color and life to the page as possible. My illustrations are full of whimsical ​details and little surprises for viewers to discover each time they look.

Outside of writing and illustrating, I’m pursuing a career in animal ​behavior research. I recently graduated with a B.S. in Ecology and ​Evolutionary Biology and a B.A. in Theatre from Rice University. ​While in college, I had the chance to work on projects ranging ​from training birds to studying how a guide dog’s early ​experiences shape its behavior. My knowledge of animals often ​finds its way into my art and stories— but blended with fun, ​fantastical elements.
